About Us...

Our Story...

Primary school teachers Krystle Ragen and Heidi Young started Blooming Beginnings in 2018 when they noticed the poor link between pre-school and kindergarten and the rough transition students were facing, especially children with special needs or social and emotional difficulties.
They also recognised that many children began school without foundation literacy and numeracy skills. This caused the students to struggle to keep up with the demands of the Australian curriculum.

When they are not teaching at the local primary school, they are preparing fun and engaging activities for Blooming Beginnings and being mums. Between them, they have over 20 years experience in the NSW public school system ranging from pre-school age to year 6. We keep our class numbers low to maximise the one-on-one opportunity for each child. Heidi is a mum of 3 and Krystle is a mum of 2 who both reside on the beautiful Central Coast of NSW.
