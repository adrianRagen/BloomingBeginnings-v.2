Introduction To School...

Introduction...

We provide students going into their first year of formal schooling with a head start by offering a taste test of what the first week of school will be like. We focus on skills that students are expected to have when they first start kindergarten. Being Kindergarten teachers ourselves, we know this is not always possible, so we provide a fast track run down of what is expected for both student and parents.
